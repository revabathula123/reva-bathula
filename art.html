Designing Art App


The team was tasked into designing a mental health app
in a way suitable for teenagers.

Center Audience: Teenagers

  • High prevalence of mental health issues: A significant percentage of teenagers experience anxiety, depression, and other mental health concerns.
  • Unique developmental stage: The teenage brain undergoes significant changes, affecting emotions, decision-making, and behavior.
  • Importance of early intervention: Addressing mental health concerns during adolescence can have long-lasting positive effects on overall well-being.
  • Increased stressors: Teenagers face academic, social, and personal pressures that can impact their mental health.
  • Reducing stigma: Promoting mental health awareness and support among teenagers helps break down barriers and encourages them to seek help.

WireFrames

Mental health education: Provide engaging lessons to teach teenagers about various mental health issues, coping strategies, and self-care techniques

Mental health calendar: Enable teenagers to track their mood, activities, and progress over time, helping them gain insights into their mental health patterns.

Assessments: Offer personalized assessments to help users understand their mental health status and identify areas where they might need support or improvement.

    Interactive discussion forum: Foster a safe and supportive community where teenagers can share experiences, ask questions, and learn from one another

    .

    Badges and rewards system: Motivate and encourage app usage by offering badges and rewards for completing lessons, participating in discussions, and reaching mental health goals

    .

    Accessible and confidential support: Ensure that our app provides a safe, judgment-free space where teenagers can openly explore and address their mental health concerns

    .
